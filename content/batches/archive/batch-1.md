## ProPGF Batch 1: Retrospective

The first Filecoin Public Goods Funding program has successfully concluded, marking a testament to the growing strength, ambition, and collaboration within the Filecoin ecosystem.

### Batch 1 Highlights

- **Total Funding Awarded**: $3,681,800
- **Projects Funded**: 14 teams
- **Applications Received**: 38 projects
- **Projects Shortlisted**: 23 projects advanced to final review

### Focus Areas

Batch 1 funded initiatives spanning:
- Core infrastructure and developer tooling
- Data retrieval and content routing services
- Storage provider implementations and monitoring
- Real-world asset integration (RWA)
- Data onboarding platforms
- SP reputation and analytics tools
- Programmable deal allocation systems
- Protocol maintenance and developer experience improvements

### Notable Projects Funded

- **FIL Ponto** ($500,000) - Public goods coordination and chain infrastructure
- **CIDgravity Gateway** ($500,000) - Data onboarding platform with S3 compatibility
- **Curio Storage** ($500,000) - Filecoin storage provider implementation
- **Filecoin Retrieval Checkers/FilCDN** ($500,000) - Retrieval assurance services
- **IPNI** ($480,000) - Content routing service linking Filecoin and IPFS
- **Filecoin Developer Tooling (FIL-B)** ($400,000) - Protocol maintenance and smart contract tooling
