# What is RetroPGF
what_is_retropgf:
  section_title: "What is RetroPGF?"
  lead: "Retroactive Public Goods Funding (RetroPGF) is a mechanism that rewards projects <strong>after</strong> they've demonstrated value to the ecosystem."
  description: "Unlike traditional grants that fund future work, RetroPGF recognizes and rewards contributions that have already been made. This approach reduces risk for funders and ensures that resources go to proven impact."
  features:
    - title: "Reward Past Impact"
      description: "Get recognized for valuable work you've already completed in the Filecoin ecosystem."
    - title: "No Milestones Required"
      description: "Unlike prospective grants, there are no deliverables to hit — your impact speaks for itself."
    - title: "Community-Driven Selection"
      description: "Projects are evaluated and voted on by the community using quadratic voting."
