Welcome to the Filecoin Community Grants blog — our new home for batch
announcements, program updates, and stories from the builders shaping the
Filecoin ecosystem.

## What you'll find here

- **Batch announcements** — opening dates, scope, and key changes for each
  ProPGF round.
- **Program updates** — selection committee composition, payout milestones, and
  process improvements.
- **Builder spotlights** — projects that have shipped meaningful work with grant
  funding.

## How to follow along

For real-time updates, join the
[FIL ProPGF Telegram group](https://t.me/+nUc-d7FXmt1kOWVl). For everything
else, this is the place to check back.

Have feedback or a topic you'd like us to cover? Reach out to **Sejal** or
**Josh** directly.
