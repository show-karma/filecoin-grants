What We're Looking For

The General Track funds projects whose work falls into (but is not limited to) the following categories:

Tooling & Developer Ecosystem

SDKs, APIs, infrastructure, and developer tools that improve the Filecoin developer experience.

Research

Technical, economic, or design research that advances the Filecoin protocol or ecosystem.

Customer Success

Initiatives that strengthen onboarding, documentation, and adoption across the ecosystem.

Governance

Mechanisms, tools, or frameworks that enhance transparency and collective decision-making.

End-User UX

Interfaces, integrations, and applications that make Filecoin more intuitive for everyday users.

New Experiments Welcome

We also invite new experiments and first-time builders, including teams from outside the Filecoin ecosystem who want to explore how their work can build on or extend Filecoin infrastructure.

If you're applying for the first time, we recommend connecting with the Filecoin PGF team (Josh & Sejal) before submitting to ensure your proposal aligns with the program's scope and goals.

For teams building on Filecoin, please make sure to check out the Filecoin Ecosystem Metrics Roundup. These are the key metrics that projects will be evaluated against, so we encourage applicants to design their work to ladder up to these metrics.
