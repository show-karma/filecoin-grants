General Track

We're thrilled to announce Filecoin ProPGF Batch 2 — a $4M milestone-based funding program designed to accelerate innovation and strengthen the Filecoin ecosystem.

Supporting projects that make Filecoin more useful, resilient, and impactful — from infrastructure and research to governance and user experience.

What We're Looking For

The General Track funds projects whose work falls into (but is not limited to) the following categories:

Tooling & Developer Ecosystem

SDKs, APIs, infrastructure, and developer tools that improve the Filecoin developer experience.

Research

Technical, economic, or design research that advances the Filecoin protocol or ecosystem.

Customer Success

Initiatives that strengthen onboarding, documentation, and adoption across the ecosystem.

Governance

Mechanisms, tools, or frameworks that enhance transparency and collective decision-making.

End-User UX

Interfaces, integrations, and applications that make Filecoin more intuitive for everyday users.

Core Dependencies

For projects that applied in ProPGF Round 1 and are considered core infrastructure or services essential to the Filecoin network.

New Experiments Welcome

We also invite new experiments and first-time builders, including teams from outside the Filecoin ecosystem who want to explore how their work can build on or extend Filecoin infrastructure.

If you're applying for the first time, we recommend joining the telegram group for FIL ProPGF before submitting to ensure your proposal aligns with the program's scope and goals.

For teams building on Filecoin, check out the Filecoin Ecosystem Metrics Roundup — the key metrics projects will be evaluated against.

Funding Model

All grants are milestone-based, with payouts tied to the completion and verification of approved deliverables.
